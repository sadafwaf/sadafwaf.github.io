How to Join the Ballas Gang

Joining the Ballas Gang isn't just about wearing purple—it’s about respect, loyalty, and earning your place in the streets. Follow the steps below to prove you’re ready.

Step 1: Contact Us

First you need to join in our discord server then go to Gang Apply Channel Submit your application and wait the gang leaders to check your application

Step 2: Show Respect

The Ballas operate on loyalty and respect. If you want in, you must prove that you understand the code.

Step 3: Prove Yourself

New recruits must complete initiation tasks. This could involve handling business, proving your skills, or standing your ground.

Step 4: Ride with the Crew

Once you pass the tests, you'll roll with the crew and earn your colors. But remember, once you're in, there's no turning back.
